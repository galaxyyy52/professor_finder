Research StatementProgram is focused on cyber-physical and complex systems with applications to mechanical, electrical, and computer systems. Professor Dullerud is particularly interested in networked, distributed and mobile cyber-physical settings. His areas of current research interest include convex optimization and machine learning in control, cyber-physical system security, cooperative robotics, stochastic simulation, and hybrid dynamical systems.
